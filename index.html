About Pensacola Locksmith

Young is one feature we won’t claim. Originally a Lock and Gunsmith shop, we’ve been in Pensacola since 1933 and many decades later, Rick and Jami Rankin and our small crew serve hundreds of local clients as well as dozens of commercial accounts (many of them household names) in the region including Gulf Breeze, Lillian, Cantonment, Milton, Orange Beach, Perdido Key and more.

We can still handle your gun needs and to learn more about that visit the Gunsmith on Garden website. Eventually we plan to sell the Locksmith business along with our awesome client base so we are separating the businesses.

We’re pretty friendly and welcome you to call, email or come in to the storefront shop at 1420 W Garden St. during opening hours, 8am to 4pm Monday - Thursday and 8am ‘till noon Friday’s. We have a really awesome cat who keeps a post out front. Her name is Lily and she’s almost as old as us, so be gentle.
