About Pensacola Locksmith

Lock and Gunsmith is a well-established locksmith company which started in 1933 and was originally located in downtown area of Pensacola. We are now on Garden St. We specialize in commercial locksmith service as well as residential. Our customers and business clients know that their accounts with us are kept in confidentiality with regards to their security. We are state vendors, property management locksmiths , corporate accounts locksmiths and maintain on going monthly service accounts. We offer mobile locksmith service as well as maintain a store front business for our keys and daily operations.

In addition to our locksmith business we maintain a gunsmith business. Rick Rankin is well known and respected for his expertise in firearms. From ballistics knowledge to repairs and custom re-barreling, Rick can do it.

We are proud of our longevity in the small business service for Pensacola and the surrounding areas. If anything our age is testimony to the fact that age has no limits as long as we do our jobs well. But we also know that our reputation was built on the trust and continued respect of our customers and for that we THANK YOU!
